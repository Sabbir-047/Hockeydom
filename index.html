We offer hockey training programs for players of all ages and skill levels, including youth, junior, and adult programs. Our training sessions are tailored to each age group's specific needs and focus on skill development, teamwork, and game strategies.

Private Lessons

Private training sessions offer personalized coaching and instruction tailored to the individual player's needs and goals. During private sessions, players receive one-on-one attention from a certified coach, allowing for focused skill development and improvement

Ski Touring

Ski touring, also known as backcountry skiing or ski mountaineering, is a form of skiing that involves traveling across snow-covered terrain using specialized equipment. Unlike traditional downhill skiing, ski touring typically involves ascending slopes under one's own power using climbing skins attached to the skis, then descending the slopes using a combination of skiing techniques
